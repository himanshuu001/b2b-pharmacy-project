Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for this scheme?

All retailers, chemists, and doctors in Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, Jammu & Kashmir, Haryana, Rajasthan, Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, and Chhattisgarh are eligible to participate.

How do I claim my rewards?

After achieving your target, complete the Gift Claim Form and submit it to your stockist. Gifts are disbursed within 30 days of complete payment.

What if I achieve a higher tier mid-year?

The scheme is annual and calculated year-on-year. You can claim rewards for your achieved tier after March 31, 2027.

Can I choose different rewards at different tiers?

Yes! Each tier offers multiple reward options (marked as "OR"). You can select based on your preference.

What about GST in the calculation?

All purchases are calculated without GST for scheme benefits. This means you earn rewards on the base medicine price only.

Is cash reimbursement available?

No, this scheme offers only product rewards. Gifts are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash.
